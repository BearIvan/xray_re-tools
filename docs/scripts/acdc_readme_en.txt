S.T.A.L.K.E.R. all.spawn compiler/decompiler for S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Clear Sky and Call of Pripyat

Decompile all.spawn:

acdc.pl -d all.spawn [-o outdir]


Compile all.spawn:

acdc.pl -c all.ltx [-f flag1[,...]] [-a level:point] [-o outfile]
