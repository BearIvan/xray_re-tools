[Introduction]
msXRayImport.dll implements support to import *.ogf, *.dm, *.skl files.
msXRayExport.dll implements support to export *.object and *.skl files.


[Installation]
Copy msXRayImport.dll and msXRayExport.dll into Milkshape 3D folder.
Adjust $sdk_root$ in xray_path.ltx and copy into the same folder.


[Usage]
Make sure to type in the extension explicitly during the export.
